benjy and will listen to your music

Have you ever wanted two people you don't know to listen to all the music you like, and then, without prompting and at length, give their own opinion on that music?

How does this work?

We have written a script to scrape data from your Spotify account. Once the data collection is complete, the website emails us your data. We will spend several minutes in person discussing your music, then manually type up our opinions and send them back to the website, which displays the results.

Who are you?

Benjy and Will

Are you affiliated with Spotify?

No. Spotify has extensive API documentation that allows developers to play with data. It also pays its artists next to nothing and has donated hundreds of millions of dollars to military contractors, and we are two college students who made this 12 hours before it was due.
